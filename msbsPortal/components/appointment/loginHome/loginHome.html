About Us

Medcare is a Private Hospital was setup in 2016 and is situated in V V Puram, Bengaluru city center. The Hospital has a capacity of 30 beds with dedicated 8 Intensive / critical care beds, 2 sophisticated operation theaters and specific dedicated OPD consultation rooms to offer the highest levels of medical care to our patients. Medcare hospital provides wide array of services with most medical, surgical specialties as well as X Ray facilities, physiotherapy, laboratory investigations and pharmacy available 24/7. The Hospital's highly specialized patient care adheres to the highest standards in serving patients from all walks of life through multi-disciplinary approach to treatment.
